Our Donate Plan

Our job is to bring a smile to the faces of helpless people. Our donation plan includes food for
the hungry people, shelter for the homeless people, and education for helpless children.

Food for a family

Our organization arranges food for helpless families. In families where there is a food crisis, we provide them with bread,pulses, rice, oil and fish. We deliver the food to their homes. This is what we want everyone to be happy.

Shelter for a family

Our organization is to provide shelter for helpless families. We provide shelter to homeless children and homeless people. We make them home. This is what we want everyone to be happy.

Education for a child

Our organization is to provide education to helpless children. Children are the assets of the future. They have to make arrangements for education because a civilized nation will be formed in the midst of education.

Help Without Assisting

Convinced that lasting help cannot be provided only from the outside, and that it is the change of the human person by getting them out of their old habits that must be operated: Akamasoa wishes to help without assisting.

Everyone Is Welcome

Located on the southeast coast of Madagascar, the Foyer de Tanjomoha was created in 1986 by Father Vincent Carme with the aim of welcoming young people with physical disabilities to treat their handicap and give them vocational training.

More information >>

Medical Support

All of the donations we receive go directly to our partners in best hospital, where the funds are used to dig wells, buy medicine and medical equipment, purchase seeds and agricultural tools, furnish classrooms with academic supplies, or build and outfit schools and hospitals.
